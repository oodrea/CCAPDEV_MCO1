Who are we?


Welcome to De La Salle University Manila's ultimate food destination! TaftFinds is dedicated to showcasing the vibrant and diverse culinary scene within and around the university campus. Whether you're a student, faculty member, or simply a food enthusiast, we are here to guide you on a mouthwatering journey through the best restaurants, cafes, and eateries that make De La Salle University Manila an epicenter of gastronomic delights. From delightful street food stalls to trendy dining spots, our platform serves as your go-to resource for discovering delectable meals, exploring unique flavors, and experiencing the rich culinary culture that thrives in the heart of Manila. Join us as we unravel the culinary treasures that await within the walls of De La Salle University Manila and beyond!
