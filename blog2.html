Affordable Food Options

Beyond Your Wallet's Reach: Exploring the Best Affordable Places

Hey there, fellow Lasallians! We all know that navigating the exciting world of university life can sometimes feel like a whirlwind of assignments, exams, and extracurricular activities. With so much
on our plates, it's no wonder that finding affordable and delicious food options around campus
often takes a backseat. We've all been there, staring at our dwindling budgets and wondering
on earth we're going to satisfy our hunger without breaking the bank. But fear not! In this guide,
we've rounded up some fantastic and pocket-friendly places to eat that will keep your stomach full
and your pockets heavy throughout your amazing Lasallian journey. So, let's dive right in and
discover these hidden gems of affordable deliciousness!

1. Potpots - Agno

PRICE: 100 Php

Welcome to the world of Potpots, where deliciousness meets healthiness! While Potpots is widely recognized for its signature crunchy and flavorful fries, walk over to the side of their stall, and there's
a delightful surprise awaiting those seeking healthier food options. If you're in the mood for a chop suey-style dish, their Mongolian rice bowl is the way to go.

At Potpots, they offer a unique pick-all-you-can concept that allows you to customize your meal according to your preferences. As you approach their stall, you'll find a selection of fresh vegetables, just waiting to be piled onto your plate. The best part? You have the freedom to select any veggies
from their list, ensuring that your meal perfectly suits your taste buds.

So, grab a small plate or a "platito" and get ready to stack your chosen veggies. Not only can you choose from a variety of vegetables, but they also provide a choice of protein. Whether you're a fan
of pork, chicken, or beef strips, this is the place for you. This interactive and DIY approach to building your meal is not only fun but also allows you to customize your portion sizes and create a well-balanced dish. Potpots has got you covered, whether you prefer pork, beef, or chicken.

2. Bacsilog - Agno

PRICE: 89 Php

Kickstart your day with a delightful breakfast from Bacsilog. While silog meals are a beloved staple in the Philippines, this particular stall offers a unique twist to this classic dish. Instead of the traditional silog you can find everywhere, this stall takes it up a notch with their creamy and savory cheese sauce, elevating the flavors to a whole new level. Prepare to be pleasantly surprised by the burst of deliciousness that the cheese sauce brings, without overpowering your taste buds. It's the perfect way to add a cheesy and flavorful touch to your morning, ensuring a mood-boosting start to your day. So, treat yourself to a cheesy breakfast delight and let the flavors linger on your palate, making it a truly memorable and satisfying experience.

3. Perico's Grill - St. La Salle Hall

PRICE Starts at 60 Php

Are you in search of a satisfying full course meal? Look no further than Pericos! While carinderia-style establishments may be uncommon in a university setting, Pericos stands out by offering a diverse range of options. Whether you're craving breakfast, lunch, or dinner, or even you find yourself longing for the comforting flavors of lutong-bahay dishes, Pericos is the perfect place for you.

At Pericos, you can expect to find a delightful assortment of ulam, showcasing the rich and authentic flavors of Filipino cuisine. From traditional favorites to regional specialties, their menu is designed to satisfy your cravings for hearty and delicious meals throughout the day. So, the next time you're in need of a complete and soul-nourishing meal, head over to Pericos. Explore their carinderia-style establishment and treat yourself to a delightful culinary experience filled with flavors that evoke feelings of warmth and nostalgia. Pericos is here to satisfy your cravings and provide a taste of home in the midst of your university journey.
